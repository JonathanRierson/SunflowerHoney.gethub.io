Our Bees-nizz is all about Honey

  • Sunflower Honey is a family run business.
  • Established in 1942, Sunflower Honey has been serving the Centeral Texas community for 78 years.
  • To produce large quantities of extra sweet honey, we use a special hybrid bee, which is half honey bee and half bumblebee.
  • Our bees obtain a majority of their pollen from a large watermelon and sunflower field, making it sweet and golden brown.
